HOW TO PLAY

Endure! Explore! Survive!

Explore and Survive

With each careful step, you edge closer to your destination, wary of the dangers that lurk in the shadows of this desolate landscape.

Use the arrow keys or W/A/S/D to move your character around the environment.

Crafting Essentials

Crafting is your key to adaptation, turning scattered remnants into tools for survival.. Use your supplies effectly.

Click on the items in the crafting menu to create medkits and molotov cocktails.

Shoot Precisely

Every click and movement counts as you navigate the perilous world of The Last of Us, honing your aim to survive against the odds.

Move your mouse to aim. Click the left mouse button to shoot. Use the right mouse button to zoom in for better accuracy.
